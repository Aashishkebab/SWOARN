Southwestern Ohio Animal Rights Network

Who we are

We are a network of animal rights activists in the Cincinatti/Dayton area that works with organizations like PETA, Anonymous for the Voiceless, and more to end all forms of animal exploitation and cruelty.

What we do

We push for change in all ways that we can, from petitioning for legislative changes to screaming our hearts out in the streets.
