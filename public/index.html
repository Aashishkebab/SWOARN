Southwestern Ohio Animal Rights Network

Who we are

We are a network of animal rights activists in the Cincinnati/Dayton area that work with organizations such as Animal Activism Mentorship, Anonymous for the Voiceless, Plant Based Treaty, Project Animal Freedom, Slaughter Free Network, PETA, and more to end all forms of animal exploitation and cruelty.

What we do, and why

Every day, billions of animals are abused and mistreated. Pigs are gassed, chicks are ground up alive, and animals are confined and exploited. We believe this is wrong.

We push for change in all ways that we can, from petitioning for legislative changes to screaming our hearts out in the streets.
